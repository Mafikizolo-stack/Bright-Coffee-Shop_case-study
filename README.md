# Bright-Coffee-Shop_case-study
The business has recently appointed a new CEO whose mission is to grow the company’s revenue and improve product performance.

### Problem Statement
The company is unable to fully leverage its sales data to optimize product performance and maximize revenue. The task is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that guide pricing, product, and timing strategies.
### Aim 
The aim of this project is to extract actionable insights from historical data and prepare a presentation to assist the CEO in decision-making.

### OBJECTIVE
Utilize data analysis, SQL, and visualization tools to enable Bright Coffee Shop to:
• Determine which products drive the highest revenue
• Identify the busiest and most profitable times of the day
• Analyze sales patterns across products and different time periods
• Develop data-driven strategies to boost overall sales performance

### Summary

A stable and high-performing business, with key opportunities in boosting customer value, refining product strategy, and maximizing underutilized periods.
